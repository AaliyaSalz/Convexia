The journey

What to expect, start to finish

Every family's path is different, but the structure is the same. Here's what happens at each stage — and who's responsible for what.

Convexia Rare Village Your physician You & your family
1
Getting started

Referral from Rare Village

Rare Village introduces your case to Convexia. You don't need a confirmed diagnosis to begin — unresolved symptoms, a suspected diagnosis, or simply not knowing your next step are all valid starting points.

What Rare Village shares

Your diagnosis or symptoms, prior testing, current doctors, your goals, and any practical constraints like cost or travel ability

What happens next

Convexia reviews the referral, confirms they can help, and reaches out to begin intake

Rare Village — makes the referral Convexia — confirms fit & begins intake
2
Getting organized

Intake & consent

Convexia collects what they need to understand your situation. You can start with whatever records you have — missing records don't block the process. Convexia will identify what additional information would help most.

What you'll share

Medical records you have, your goals — therapy, trial, diagnosis, or specialist — and practical constraints like cost, geography, or caregiver capacity

Consent

You formally agree to share your information before anything begins. You can ask questions, take your time, and withdraw at any point

Your data

Never used to train AI models. Only the people working directly on your case can access your records

Output

A structured intake file with your goals, available records, what's missing, and your clinician contacts

You — share records & goals Convexia — organizes the case Your physician — provides records when available
3
Behind the scenes

Structuring your case

Convexia organizes everything you've shared into a structured profile — building a timeline of your disease, mapping your genetics and symptoms, and identifying what data is missing that might change your options.

Disease timeline

Onset, progression, major events, prior diagnoses, and treatments organized chronologically

Genetics & phenotype

Your genetic variants and symptoms mapped into structured terms the analysis can search against

Missing-data map

A clear list of what information gaps exist and which ones — if filled — could open up new options

Practical constraints

Cost, travel ability, trial willingness, and caregiver capacity built into the profile so recommendations reflect your real situation

Convexia — structures the profile Your physician — clarifies ambiguous information
4
The search

Evidence triage

Convexia's AI searches a much wider landscape than any single physician has time to cover — looking across known therapies, drug repurposing possibilities, active clinical trials, specialist referrals, and practical support resources.

Therapies

Known treatments and mechanism-linked options relevant to your disease

Drug repurposing

Drugs approved for other conditions that may have relevance to yours

Clinical trials

Active trials — including ones not widely publicized — that you may qualify for

Next tests

High-yield tests that could open up new diagnostic or treatment options

Specialists

Clinician types or institutions that should be part of your care

Support resources

Advocacy groups, financial assistance, peer communities, and care navigation

Convexia — runs the search & generates triage report
5
The decision point

Actionability classification

Based on the triage, Convexia classifies your case into one of two paths. This is not a judgment on your disease — it reflects what the current evidence supports and what's most useful for you right now.

✓  Near-term actionable

A potential therapy, trial, specialist, or high-yield test has been identified. Your case is ready to review with you and your physician.

→  Deeper work required

The first-pass search isn't sufficient to make responsible suggestions yet. A deeper analysis is needed — and Convexia will do it.

85%

of cases require deeper work — this is expected and normal, not a setback. It means your case is complex enough to deserve more thorough analysis before any next step is suggested.

Convexia — classifies & explains the path
6
Making sense of it

Report review meeting

Convexia walks you through the findings in plain language — no jargon. This meeting is for you and your family. The goal is to leave with clarity and a concrete plan for what comes next.

What gets covered

What was found, what it means in plain language, what the next steps are, and who is responsible for each one

What you leave with

A shared navigation plan with named owners, open questions prepared for your physician, and resource connections where helpful

Convexia — leads the meeting Rare Village — supports & connects resources You — ask everything Your physician — reviews & decides next steps

After the report

Your pathway forward

Your case is routed into the pathway — or combination of pathways — that makes the most sense. These aren't mutually exclusive; many families pursue more than one at once.

💊

Therapy

Your physician evaluates a known or mechanism-linked therapy option identified in the triage

🔬

Clinical trial

You are screened against active trials and any eligibility gaps are worked through

🧬

Testing

Your clinician orders high-yield genetic, biomarker, or specialty testing that could change your options

👩‍⚕️

Specialist

You are connected to a specialist in genetics, neurology, metabolism, or immunology who can take the work further

🤝

Resources

You are connected to advocacy groups, financial support, peer communities, and care navigation

📋

Deep dive

Convexia performs deeper literature, mechanism, therapy, and trial analysis for complex cases

🔄

Longitudinal monitoring

Your case stays active. As new data emerges — labs, trials, symptoms — Convexia refreshes the analysis

What Convexia will and won't do

Understanding the boundaries clearly protects you and sets the right expectations from the start.

✓  Convexia will

Collect and organize your records and history
Search broadly for therapies, trials, testing options, and specialists
Identify what information is missing and what it could unlock
Walk you through findings in plain language
Connect you to advocacy, financial, and peer resources
Track your case over time and refresh as new data emerges
Keep your data private — never used for AI training

✗  Convexia will not

Diagnose your disease
Prescribe or recommend a specific treatment
Order labs, imaging, or genetic testing
Tell you whether a therapy is right for you
Replace your physician or manage your clinical care
Promise a specific outcome from the analysis

You don't have to navigate this alone.

Rare Village and Convexia are here to help you see what's possible — and to make sure the right people are in the room when decisions get made.
